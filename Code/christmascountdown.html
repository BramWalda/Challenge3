About Northpole

Welcome to Santa's only Elf Outpost in Cork
Santa’s head Elf has been secretly looking around Cork for a private outpost to make sure the boys and girls of Cork get their toys in time for Christmas. He has also recruited a merry band of real Cork Elves to run the show.
